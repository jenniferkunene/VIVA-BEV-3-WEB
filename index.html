About Us

The VIVA story began in 2013 in the industrial hub of the Kingdom of Eswatini, Matsapha, when we first employed reverse osmosis to purify municipal water, bottle it and sell to the public. Having just been producing purified still water since then until 2024, today we are priviledged to boast of a wider range of water products as we have, in 2021, added sparkling & flavoured water to our product list and expansive footprint across the Kingdom of Eswatini.

From our factory in Matsapha we supply and distribute to wholesalers, retailers, companies and individuals the full selection of our products. Striving for consistency in our products and efficiency in our business; the finest quality products, presented in the highest quality packaging, we take tremendous pride in customer service and our unsurpassed consistent safety, quality, good taste and convinience
